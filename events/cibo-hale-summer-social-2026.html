Event support

Inspire AV

Photography, PA and technical support for the Summer Social were provided by Inspire AV.

Inspire works alongside interior designers, architects and homeowners to deliver discreet smart-home technology, lighting design, home cinema and integrated AV throughout Cheshire, Yorkshire and London.

For selected collaborative projects, Inspire can also help design partners capture completed spaces through photography, video and shared case studies.
