Who We Are

Built for the industry, by the industry

Interior Girls Club is a welcoming, open community for interior designers, decorators, architects, suppliers, stylists, and everyone in between. No gatekeeping — just genuine connection and growth.

We believe that when women in the interior world lift each other up, extraordinary things happen. Share your work, find collaborators, attend events, and be part of something bigger.

The Founders

The women behind the club

Interior Girls Club was founded by two passionate designers who wanted to create the community they wished had existed when they were starting out — real, honest, and full of heart.

Sally O'Connor
Co-Founder · Principal Designer, Hesellic Design

Sally is the founder of Hesellic Design — a sophisticated studio renowned for blending storytelling, emotion, and personal branding into beautifully curated spaces. Her work artfully integrates traditional elements with contemporary twists, guided by a profound appreciation for architecturally defined interiors.

Charlotte
Co-Founder · Award-Winning Interior Designer

Charlotte has a relentless passion for turning houses into homes — creating timeless, custom interiors that achieve the 'wow' factor while remaining beautifully liveable. Specialising in luxury residential and commercial spaces, she's renowned for the dedicated, personal service that keeps clients coming back, building long-standing relationships up and down the country.
